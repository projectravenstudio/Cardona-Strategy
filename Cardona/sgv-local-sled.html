Why the San Gabriel Valley?

The San Gabriel Valley is home to 30+ diverse cities ranging from small, tight-knit communities to mid-sized municipalities with complex service delivery needs.

SGV cities share common challenges: balancing development with community character, modernizing aging infrastructure, navigating inter-departmental coordination, managing limited budgets, and responding to evolving resident expectations.

Our Local SLED Initiative for the San Gabriel Valley brings local professionals who understand these dynamics—from the culture of your community to the practical realities of municipal operations.
